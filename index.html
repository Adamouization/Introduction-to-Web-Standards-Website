This general introductory guide aims to introduce the reader to the role of web standards in the World Wide Web. To grasp a general understanding of web standards, it is necessary to understand:

  • What they are,
  • How they evolved to standardise the World Wide Web,
  • Who manages them,
  • What is the W3C,
  • How new standards are created, and
  • What are the most important web standards.
